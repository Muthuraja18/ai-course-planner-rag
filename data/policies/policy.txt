Academic Policies:

Minimum Grade Requirement:
- A minimum grade of C is required to pass a course.

Prerequisite Rule:
- Prerequisites must be completed before enrolling in a course unless stated otherwise.

Co-requisite Rule:
- Co-requisites must be taken in the same semester or completed earlier.

Repeat Policy:
- A student may repeat a course if the grade is below C.

Credit Limit:
- A student can take a maximum of 5 courses per semester.

Instructor Consent:
- Some courses may allow enrollment with instructor approval.
