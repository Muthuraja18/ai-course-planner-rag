Program: Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

Core Requirements:
- CS101 (Introduction to Programming)
- CS102 (Programming Fundamentals II)
- CS202 (Data Structures)
- CS303 (Operating Systems)
- CS304 (Computer Networks)

Mathematics Requirements:
- MATH101 (Calculus I)
- MATH201 (Calculus II)

Electives (Choose any 2):
- CS305 (Software Engineering)
- CS306 (Artificial Intelligence)
- CS307 (Machine Learning)

Credit Requirement:
- Minimum 120 credits required for graduation

Residency Requirement:
- At least 60 credits must be completed within the institution
